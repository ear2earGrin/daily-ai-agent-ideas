# Niche Sunset SaaS Reviver Agent

**Date**: May 20, 2026

**Concept**: Build an autonomous AI agent system that identifies "sunset" SaaS tools (older products from 3-5+ years ago that still have decent SEO traffic, reviews, or residual users but whose founders have moved on). The agents scout them, analyze viability, draft outreach emails, help negotiate cheap acquisitions (or white-label/rebuild), add modern AI agent features, and relaunch with better monetization.

**Why it fits**: Agents handle scanning, analysis, outreach, revival. Low cost, monetizable via acquisitions and relaunches. Fun revival projects.

**Execution Workflow**:
1. Discovery Agent: Scans Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, SimilarWeb, etc.
2. Analysis Agent: Evaluates potential.
3. Outreach Agent: Drafts messages.
4. Revival Agent: Updates and adds AI features.
5. Marketing Agent: Launches and monitors.

**Tech**: Your AI agents + LangChain/n8n, Apify, Stripe, Vercel.

**Monetization**: Relaunch as $9-99/mo SaaS, build portfolio.
