## Idea for June 20, 2026

**Idea: AI-Powered Niche Trend Reporter**

**Description:** An autonomous AI agent system that scans X (Twitter), Reddit, and news for emerging trends in a specific niche (e.g., indie game dev, sustainable fashion). It generates daily/weekly reports, visual summaries, and even starter content or product ideas. Monetize via subscriptions ($9/mo for premium reports), affiliate links, or selling custom agents.

**Why solo with AI:** Use LangGraph or CrewAI for multi-agent orchestration: one for scanning, one for analysis, one for generation. Deploy on Vercel or Replit with cron jobs.

**Monetization:** Freemium model, Gumroad sales, or integrate Stripe.

**Fun factor:** High - discover cool stuff daily, potential viral on X.
